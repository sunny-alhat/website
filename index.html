About

Meraki Therapy Center is a wellness facility focused on helping people improve their mental and emotional health. They provide various therapies and counseling services that are customized to fit the individual needs of each client.

Project Requirements

The overall design and tone of the website was supposed to be simple, easy to navigate, and welcoming. This approach was essential because visitors were seeking support for their mental and emotional well-being.

So had to create a website that provides comforting atmosphere to encourage users to explore the services offered and feel at ease while seeking help.
